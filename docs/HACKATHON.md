## Judging Format

Expo-style: judges come to your table in 3 rounds. Each visit is ~4 minutes. Be ready to demo live and answer technical questions.

## Tracks We Are Competing In

### 1. NextEra Energy — Malware Analysis Challenge (primary)
Explicit ask: AI-driven threat detection and response with sandboxed execution.

Your demo must answer these three questions about the sample:
1. What type of malware is it and what does it do?
2. What systems and data are at risk?
3. What are the containment and elimination steps?

Required capabilities to show:
- Sandboxed virtual environment that safely contains the malware
- Automated pipeline that extracts behavioral and structural identifiers
- Mitigation and remediation plan generation

### 2. Best AI Hack — General Track
Prize: Snaptain S5C Elite 1080p Drone. Our multi-agent ADK orchestration qualifies.

### 3. Google Cloud ADK Challenge — "Building a Self-Healing World with Google ADK"
Secondary track. Our Zeus/Athena/Hades/Apollo/Ares agent hierarchy qualifies.

## General Judging Rubric

| Criterion | What Judges Look For |
|---|---|
| Innovation | Novel approach to the problem |
| Technical implementation | Working, non-trivial system |
| Design | UX and presentation quality |
| Beginner-friendliness | Accessibility and clarity of the project |
